## üìù Basic Usage (3 Steps)

### Step 1: Enter Product Details
1. Select **Product Category** from dropdown (e.g., "Electronics & Computers")
2. Enter **Product Value** in dollars (e.g., 5000)
3. Enter **Weight** in kilograms (e.g., 25.5)

### Step 2: Select Countries & Shipping
1. Choose **Origin Country** (e.g., China)
2. Choose **Destination Country** (e.g., United States)
3. Select **Shipping Method** (e.g., Air Express)

### Step 3: Calculate
1. Click **Calculate** button
2. View detailed breakdown
3. Optionally **Save** or export to **PDF**

## üìä Understanding Results

### Cost Breakdown
- **Product Value (FOB)**: Your purchase price
- **Shipping & Insurance**: Freight costs
- **Import Duty**: Tax on imported goods (% varies)
- **VAT/GST**: Value-added tax (% varies by country)
- **Handling Fees**: Processing charges
- **Total Landed Cost**: FINAL TOTAL to pay

### Key Terms
- **FOB**: Free On Board (product cost only)
- **HS Code**: Harmonized System product code
- **Duty**: Import tax based on product type
- **VAT/GST**: Sales tax applied by destination country
- **Landed Cost**: Total cost to receive goods
